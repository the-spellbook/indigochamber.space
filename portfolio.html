"Het Dorp" by Wim Sonneveld interpretation

march 17th, 2025

for a HKU assignment we had to reinterpret a piece of music selected from a list of classics, with the intention of becoming aware of our creative artistry and individuality. i chose to reinterpret Het Dorp by Wim Sonneveld, making a more intense, aggressive and melancholic version inspired by early shoegaze and psychedelia. highlighting my roots in dreampop and shoegaze, i used the yearning for the past apparent in the song to connect the threads from the french original to modern alternative pop music.
