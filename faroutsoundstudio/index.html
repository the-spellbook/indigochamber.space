Welcome to Far Out Sound Studio, a cozy analog studio in Rotterdam...

Far Out Sound Studio, formely known as Duct Tape Studios, is an renowed analog recording studio located in Rotterdam. In the very center works Niek van den Driesschen, a engineer, musician and analog freak.
A passionate collector of vintage gear since childhood, his expertise and creative spirit makes him a sought-after collaborator and engineer in the local scene and beyond.

The studio simply has a great sound, a vocal booth and seperate mixing room, and a hybrid analog/digital recording setup, and loads of rare gear. It's a playground where you can fully indulge in toying with tape, experiment with rare microphones, and reamp your recordings through as many quirky devices as your heart desires.
